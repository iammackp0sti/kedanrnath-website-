Our Story & Spirit of Hospitality

Frontier House (Posti Heritage Inn) is a family-owned and operated sanctuary in Kedarnath. Nestled amidst the majestic, snow-capped Himalayas, we combine honest mountain values with a peaceful atmosphere to welcome weary pilgrims, families, and solo travellers.

Managed directly by the Posti family, we believe in treating every guest like a member of our home. After a testing trek up the mountains, find tranquility in our warm rooms, enjoy running hot water, rest easy under deep blankets, and wake up to the sound of the Mandakini River.

Located near Gol Chowk, we are just a short walk to the sacred Kedarnath Temple. Our team is always ready to guide you on local weather conditions, temple timing, and travel logistics.

Guiding Your Kedarnath Yatra

To make your journey smooth and spiritually rewarding, here are essential tips for planning your stay and darshan at Kedarnath.

Temple Darshan Timings

The Kedarnath temple opens daily at 4:00 AM for VIP / General Puja, closes from 3:00 PM to 5:00 PM, and opens again for evening Aarti before closing at 9:00 PM. Plan your walking start early.

Rapid Weather Shifts

Temperatures can drop below freezing even in summer afternoons. Always pack heavy woollen clothing, windcheaters, thermals, and dynamic raincoats. We provide extra blankets in your room.

Trek & Helipad Transport

The trek starts from Gaurikund (16 km steep walk). If you fly via helicopter, the helipad is just 500 meters from Frontier House, making check-in simple for elders and families.
