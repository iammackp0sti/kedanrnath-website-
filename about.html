Our Story

A Family Welcome in the Heart of Kedarnath

The Spirit of Yatra

Creating Comfort During the Sacred Journey

Frontier House sits quietly amid the tranquil beauty of Kedarnath, offering tired travelers and devoted pilgrims a comfortable place to rest after their long, intense journey.

Many pilgrims struggle to find clean, reliable, and comfortable accommodations during the busy yatra seasons. In high altitudes, simple details like warm mattresses, generator power backups, and private western toilets become essential shelters. We created Frontier House to bridge this gap, offering honest service, comfortable family-friendly spaces, and peaceful surroundings.

Mountain Culture

More Than a Stay, A Family Welcome

Frontier House is family-run, and every single guest is welcomed with genuine care and dedication. Our goal is straightforward: to make every visitor feel comfortable, safe, and looked after like a member of our family. From checking on trek route safety to helping elder pilgrims settle in with hot water, we strive to be a helpful guiding resource during your sacred yatra.
